Thespians

The Thespians are a junior society of enthusiastic actors and singers who go to festivals and meet with other Thespians. At these festivals the Thespians compete and perform.


Current Musical

This year's musical is none other than a children's favorite: Descendants. Descendants is a fan favorite movie for many children because of its great characters and joyful acting. The musical will take place on Thursday, March 12th at 6pm and Friday, March 13th at 7pm, and everyone is invited!


Past Musicals

We have had many musicals performed over the years. A few of our past musicals include Matilda, Shrek, and more. Last year we had Matilda, which was played by some amazing actors, and Shrek, which as seen in the image above, is played by Charlie.

What is Theater

Theater is a place where everyone can put all their love for acting onto the stage and perform wonderful musicals for both friends and family. The theater is run by Mr. Aiello, and the musicals are heavily student-run, which might seem like chaos, but it goes very well and every year a new great musical is performed.
